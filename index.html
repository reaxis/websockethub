Websockethub.com

Painless chatroom widgets on your website

Welcome to websockethub.com. Try out the library in the chatwidget on the right side of the screen.

To include a chatroom widget in your own page, put this in your HTML: 

<script src="https://rawgit.com/hraban/chatroomwidget/master/chatroomwidget.js"></script>

Requires jQuery.
